## Why

RestroFood is the closest vendored competitor to Carte's v0.1 scope because it bundles menu browsing, WooCommerce ordering, and reservation-like delivery scheduling into one plugin. We need an evidence-grounded teardown so Milestone 0 can compare its all-in-one architecture to Carte's planned package split.

## What Changes

Author `docs/competitive-analysis/restrofood.md` with the required sections on domain coordination, admin information architecture, frontend rendering, strengths, and weaknesses. The doc will cite the vendored RestroFood source at specific file:line locations and stay within the clean-room quoting limits.
